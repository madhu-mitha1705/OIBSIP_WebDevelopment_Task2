Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

"Dream is not that which you see while sleeping, it is something that does not let you sleep."

Biography

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, known as the Missile Man of India, was a visionary scientist and the 11th President of India. His work on the development of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology earned him a name in the Indian Defense Research. A man of wisdom, humility, and inspiration, Dr. Kalam touched millions with his dedication to education, innovation, and youth empowerment.
